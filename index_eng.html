    Cloud Co-work Solution

    cube Story

    Story
    It is a space where you can put all information generated for your business in card form. this is a collaborative space or a business space where members can share and communicate data by story sharing
    Card
    All digital information is stored in the form of a card. All cards are easy to add, move, manage and communicate intuitively. In particular, the management cards show the progress of the work at a glance
    Card Item
    The card consists of information items such as text, check box, file, image, video, schedule, and function items such as mail, electronic approval, chat and so on. There are also management items such as workflow and progress.
    Chat
    All stories enable chatting among shared members, which improves work efficiency by enabling active and prompt communication as well as information sharing for work and business
    Work Status
    Analyze the progress of business in real time through management cards such as checkboxes, workflows, workflow counters, and progress. The KANBAN board view provides a clear visual representation of what to do, and what has been done.
    Story Template
    For repetitive tasks, you can register your story template to efficiently reproduce your work. You can use it as a manual or a procedure by using a read-only story.
